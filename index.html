The Initiative

Women4Vibecoding Europe is a pan-European initiative where professional women design, build, and deploy AI-powered tools. Our intensive build sprints incorporate practical awareness of the EU AI Act directly into the development process.

Why This Matters

Driving Economic Opportunity

  • Bridging the Labor Gap
  • Empowering Entrepreneurs
  • Tangible Productivity Gains

Addressing Costs and Barriers

  • The Leadership Gap
  • The Adoption Gap
  • Risk Mitigation

What Is Vibecoding

Vibecoding is a practical, AI-assisted way of building digital products without relying on traditional coding paths. It focuses on:

  • Logic and structure
  • Strategic decision-making
  • AI as a collaborative partner

Who This Is For

Professional women across policy, technology, business, research, and communications who want to build with AI responsibly.

What We Build

  • AI assistants and automation workflows
  • Compliance-aware tools for the European regulatory landscape
  • Dashboards and early-stage AI products

Support gender inclusion in applied AI and European capacity building.
